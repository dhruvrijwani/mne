Everything you need to know about India's most comprehensive 11-month Media & Entertainment program — from eligibility and fees to what the Capstone Project involves and where graduates work.

01What's the duration of this media and entertainment course?
This media and entertainment course is an 11-month full-time program. The structure includes 9 months of intensive classroom training followed by 2 months dedicated to an industry Capstone Project. Unlike traditional media courses that span two years, this entertainment management course is designed for faster career entry while maintaining comprehensive coverage of all essential specializations.
02What specializations are covered in this post graduate program?
This mass media and communication course covers all 11 critical specializations: Advertising & PR, Film Marketing & Distribution, OTT & New-Age Media, Digital & Social Media, Celebrity & Sports Management, Event Management, Media Planning & Buying, Media Budgeting & Finance, Integrated Marketing Communications, Brand & Marketing Management, and Content Marketing. Every module is designed to build both domain expertise and management capabilities.
03Where is this media communication course located?
This program is at WeSchool's Mumbai campus in Matunga, positioned right at the center of India's media and entertainment industry. Being in Mumbai means your guest lecturers are working professionals, your live projects involve actual brands, and your industry visits happen with the companies where you'll eventually work.
04What's the program fee?
The program fee is ₹4 lakh + GST for the complete 11-month program. This includes all classroom training, live projects, industry visits, guest lectures, workshops, and the two-month Capstone Project. EMI options are available through education finance partners.
05Is this a traditional masters in media and entertainment?
This is not a traditional 2-year master's degree. This is an intensive 11-month industry-focused program designed for rapid skill-building and career entry. Unlike conventional media courses that emphasize academic research, this program prioritizes practical application, live projects, and portfolio development.
06What careers can I pursue after this post graduation?
After completing this program, you're prepared for diverse roles: Marketing Manager, Media Planner, Brand Strategist, Content Producer, Campaign Manager, PR Manager, Social Media Lead, Film Marketing Executive, Celebrity Manager, and Sports Marketing Analyst. Graduates work in advertising agencies, film production companies, OTT platforms, media planning firms, PR agencies, brand management divisions, and more.
07Does this program include practical work or just theory?
This program is fundamentally application-based. Throughout the course, you'll work on live brand projects, analyze real campaign case studies, attend guest lectures from industry practitioners, visit companies, participate in workshops, and complete a comprehensive 2-month Capstone Project with an actual company. You graduate with a portfolio of executed work, not just theoretical knowledge.
08Can non-media graduates apply?
Yes, this program accepts graduates from any stream with minimum 50% marks. The curriculum is designed to train you across all 11 specializations regardless of whether your undergraduate degree was in commerce, engineering, arts, science, or mass media. Many successful students come from non-media backgrounds. What matters is your interest in building a professional career in media and entertainment.
09How does this program prepare me for the industry?
This program prepares you through a unique blend of domain expertise, management skills, analytics capabilities, and AI applications. The curriculum combines creative specializations like film marketing and content creation with business fundamentals like media budgeting, financial planning, and ROI analysis. By the end, you're industry-ready with proven capabilities — not just educated about media.
10What happens during the Capstone Project?
The Capstone Project is the culminating experience where you spend the final two months working directly with a real company on an actual industry challenge. You'll take ownership of a complete project, work under company mentorship, manage real deadlines and stakeholder expectations, execute work at professional standards, and create portfolio pieces that demonstrate your capabilities to future employers.
11Are there placement opportunities after this program?
Yes, graduates have strong placement outcomes. Alumni work at Yash Raj Films, Zee Entertainment, Google India, Delhi Capitals, Reliance Digital, CNBC TV18, Facebook, Viacom18, Ogilvy, Balaji Telefilms, Reliance Jio, Times Network, Wizcraft, and Lintas. The program connects you directly with the industry through live projects, guest lectures, company visits, and the Capstone Project — all of which create placement pathways.
12Why study in Mumbai versus other cities?
Mumbai is India's media and entertainment capital, home to film production houses, advertising agencies, OTT platforms, television networks, and PR firms. Being located here means your guest lecturers are currently working in the industry, your live projects involve actual Mumbai-based brands, and your Capstone Project connects you directly with the professional ecosystem where careers are built.
13How does this program integrate management and analytics?
This is an integrated marketing program that blends creativity with management, analytics, and AI. While learning domain skills like film marketing and OTT strategy, you simultaneously learn business fundamentals like media budgeting, financial planning, ROI analysis, consumer behavior, and data-driven decision making. The program produces media managers — not just media practitioners — who can manage campaigns, budgets, teams, and outcomes.
