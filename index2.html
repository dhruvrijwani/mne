Everything you need to know about India's most comprehensive 11-month Media & Entertainment program — from eligibility and fees to what the Capstone Project involves and where graduates work.

01What's the duration of this media and entertainment course?
This media and entertainment course is an 11-month full-time program. The mass media and communication course structure includes 9 months of intensive classroom training followed by 2 months dedicated to an industry Capstone Project. Unlike traditional media courses that span two years, this entertainment management course is designed for faster career entry while maintaining comprehensive coverage of all essential specializations.
02What specializations are covered in this mass media and communication course?
This mass media and communication course covers all 11 critical specializations: Advertising & PR, Film Marketing & Distribution, OTT & New-Age Media, Digital & Social Media, Celebrity & Sports Management, Event Management, Media Planning & Buying, Media Budgeting & Finance, Integrated Marketing Communications, Brand & Marketing Management, and Content Marketing.
03Where is this media communication course located?
This media communication course is located at WeSchool's Mumbai campus in Matunga, positioned right at the center of India's media and entertainment industry. Being in Mumbai means your guest lecturers are working professionals, your live projects involve actual brands, and your industry visits happen with the companies where you'll eventually work.
04What's the program fee for this entertainment management course?
The entertainment management course fee is ₹4 lakh + GST for the complete 11-month program. This investment includes all classroom training, live projects, industry visits, guest lectures, workshops, and the two-month Capstone Project. EMI options are available through education finance partners.
05Can non-media graduates apply for this program?
Yes, this media and communication program accepts graduates from any stream with minimum 50% marks. The mass media and communication course is designed to train you across all 11 specializations regardless of whether your undergraduate degree was in commerce, engineering, arts, science, or mass media. The curriculum starts with foundational concepts, so prior media education isn't required.
06Does this program include practical work or just theory?
This media courses program is fundamentally application-based. Throughout the program you'll work on live brand projects, analyze real campaign case studies, attend guest lectures from industry practitioners, visit companies, participate in hands-on workshops, and complete a comprehensive 2-month Capstone Project with an actual company. Every trimester includes live projects with real deadlines and real stakes.
07Where do students get jobs after this program?
Our post graduates have taken jobs with Jio Studios, YRF, Balaji Telefilms, Eros International, Facebook, Endemol, Wizcraft, Viacom18, Times of India, T-Series, Omnicom Media Group, Clea PR, Google India, Delhi Capitals, Reliance Digital, CNBC TV18, and many more leading media organizations.
08What happens during the Capstone Project?
The Capstone Project is the culminating experience where you spend the final two months working directly with a real company on an actual industry challenge. You'll take ownership of a complete project, work under company mentorship, manage real deadlines, and create portfolio pieces that demonstrate your capabilities to future employers. Many graduates cite their Capstone Project as the deciding factor in their placement interviews.
09What is the market value of this program?
The postgraduate program in Media and Entertainment from Welingkar Institute holds significant market value. The program's track record of 16+ successful batches, a strong alumni base, and WeSchool's association with SP Mandali — a renowned 140-year-old educational society — all contribute to its credibility. The institute is consistently ranked among the top 100 institutions in NIRF and is the No. 1 Private Business School in Mumbai.
10Is this a traditional master's degree in media?
This is not a traditional 2-year master's degree. This post graduation in mass media and communication is an intensive 11-month industry-focused program designed for rapid skill-building and career entry. Unlike conventional programs that emphasize academic research, this course prioritizes practical application, live projects, and portfolio development — built around what the industry actually needs.
